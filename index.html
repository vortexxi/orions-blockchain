ORIONS
Cryptocurrency economy
Orions is a cryptocurrency that works as a medium of exchange in the economy of the same name. Its objective is to facilitate and promote exchanges of goods and services for a small community of highly qualified members in their respective areas of expertise and knowledge.
Orions token works on Etherum's ECR-20 network and only 100,000 were minted on the Fuse.io platform on 10/21/2020 while the Orionids could be observed in the sky.
